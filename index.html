Business Makes LA County Work Awards

Executive speakers, civic leadership, and business advocacy stakeholders gathered in Downtown Los Angeles for BizFed’s inaugural county-wide recognition experience.

Civic & Enterprise Awards ExperienceDistricts 1–5

Bizzi Awards Production

The Challenge

As Los Angeles County emerged from the COVID-19 pandemic, BizFed launched its inaugural Business Makes LA County Work Awards to recognize businesses, organizations, and public officials driving economic recovery and community impact across all five supervisorial districts. The live Downtown Los Angeles experience brought together elected officials, chamber leaders, advocacy organizations, and business stakeholders representing a network of more than 215 business groups, 410,000 companies, and 5 million employees across the county.

Our Approach

Let’s Play Ultimate Events led full-scale production coordination and show execution for the inaugural Bizzi Awards experience, integrating prerecorded supervisor introductions and recipient acceptance segments into a cohesive live event environment designed to celebrate civic leadership, small business advocacy, and community resilience.
